About Us

what we do

Design for life

Peafowl Interiors was formed not as a business, but with an intent and sheer passion to create a new living space for ourselves. Later we realised the pleasure in sharing that expertise to others homes also, for a commercial gain.

Now, we, Peafowl Interiors is known as one of the best interior designing company in Bangalore. At Peafowl, we are proud of ourselves in exceeding the focus of interior design by distributing lavish and trendy homes that flawlessly match to our client’s needs and dreams. We have vast experience in all sorts of interior designing and our expert designers are best in office designs, residential designs and commercial interior designing. We take time and study the customer needs and apply their needs in our designing with a unique innovative idea for the superior look and eye-catching design.
